Mounting

Recommended tracker position

  • Waist: on the side above waist or in the centre of the waist depending on your build (around the level of your belly button).
  • Chest: facing forward middle of the chest or upper chest.
  • Hip: facing forward on your hip (where you put your belt).
  • Leg: right above the knee or higher up the thigh depending on your build (facing forward).
  • Ankle: on the ankle (facing any direction you want).
  • Foot: top of the foot. Tracker’s “up” facing towards ankle, and “front” facing up.
  • Elbow: on lower arm, inwards, close to elbow.

If possible, put trackers/straps directly on skin (under your shirt) for better adhesion

Where to place trackers

  • 5 trackers: waist (standing) OR chest (lying down), knees and ankles.
  • 6 trackers: waist, chest, knees and ankles.
  • 7 trackers: waist, chest, hip, knees and ankles.
  • 8 trackers: waist, chest, knees, ankles and feet.
  • 9 trackers: waist, chest, hip, knees, ankles and feet.
  • 10 trackers: waist, chest, knees, ankles, feet and elbows.
  • 11 trackers: waist, chest, hip, knees, ankles, feet and elbows.
