Mounting

Recommended tracker position

  • Chest: facing forward middle of the chest or upper chest.
  • Waist: on the side above hip or in the centre of the belly depending on your build (around the level of your belly button).
  • Hip: facing forward on your hip (where you put your belt).
  • Upper Leg: right above the knee or higher up the thigh depending on your build. Facing forward.
  • Lower leg: on the ankle (facing any direction you prefer).
  • Foot: top of the foot.
  • Lower arm: on lower arm, inwards, close to elbow.
  • Upper arm: on upper arm, on the side.
  • Shoulder: good luck.

If possible, put trackers/straps directly on skin (under your shirt) for better adhesion

Where to place trackers

  • 5 trackers: chest, upper legs and lower legs.
  • 6 trackers: waist, chest, upper legs and lower legs.
  • 7 trackers: waist, chest, hip, upper legs and lower legs.
  • 8 trackers: waist, chest, upper legs, lower legs and feet.
  • 9 trackers: waist, chest, hip, upper legs, lower legs and feet.
  • 10 trackers: waist, chest, upper legs, lower legs, feet and elbows.
  • 11 trackers: waist, chest, hip, upper legs, lower legs, feet and elbows.
