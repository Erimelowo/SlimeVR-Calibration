Frequent Issues

My trackers go the wrong direction

  • Make sure IMU_ROTATION in firmware is correct.
  • Make sure your mounting orientation in the server is correct. (you might have to lie about them for certain setups)
  • If it’s only off by a few degrees, shift your trackers inwards or outwards a bit, then full reset.

My avatar floats above the ground

  • Increase your user real height in VRChat.

My feet trackers are on my avatar’s knees

    This is an issue with VRChat's current IK.

  • Use IKTweaks OR VRChat's Beta IK and select the “height” scaling mode the settings.

My knees don’t bend

  • Make sure you have knee trackers above your knees and assigned as knee trackers.
  • Make sure your ankle trackers are set as ankles and are on your ankles, not feet.
  • Make sure VRChat is binding your feet to your feet trackers, not knee trackers.

My feet go through the floor/my feet are sliding/tracking isn’t precise enough, not 1:1/etc...

  • If you are using 5 trackers, get a sixth tracker.
  • Make sure your body proportions are configured properly: calibration page

My legs cross when sitting down

  • Try mounting your knee trackers more inwards.
  • Try mounting your knee trackers higher on your thighs or lower on your knees depending on your build.
  • Calibrate with your legs straight and a normal hip width (24-32) in your body proportions.
  • Use fast reset to correct leg crossing: assigning a keybind for resetting

My hips shifts when lifting my legs

  • Make sure your waist tracker is on your waist, above your hips.
